Passport System

Passport system:

No. (2) For the year 2011
Article 1 - every Iraqi can obtain a passport.
Article 2 - there are four types of passports: -
First - Regular passport according to the form (1) attached to this system. 
Second - Service passport according to the form No. (2) Attached to this system.
Third - Special passport according to the form (3) attached to this system.
Fourth - Diplomatic passport according to the form (4) attached to this system.
Article 3 -
First - Passport in its four kinds consists of (48) page except the cover, the dimensions are (9) Cm. width and (12,5) Cm. length, the color of the pages is Green conqueror and in mediates of all the pages of the passport the Emblem of the Republic of Iraq.
Second - in the center of the passport cover of the emblem of the Republic of Iraq.
Third - the color of the cover of the passport as follows: -
A - Regular / Dark Blue.
B - Service / Dark Red.
C - Special / Dark Green.
D - Diplomatic / Ruby Red.

Article 4 - A person who requests the passport must be: -
First - Iraqi nationality.
Second - is prohibited from leaving the race in accordance with the laws in force.
Third - Sane, unless he travels for the purpose of health treatment with a legal guardian.
Article 5 - Minister of the Interior authorized passports officer the power to issue passport and visa.
Article 6 -
First - person who requests passport must attends in front of the competent officer passports and fill out the form prepared for this purpose.
Second - General Director of Nationality office can, in cases of extreme necessity, excuse the applicant for a passport that cannot attend from attendance in front of the passport officer.
Article 7 -
First - Passport have a validity period of (8) eight years from the date of issuance for person who reached (15) fifteen years of age or older.
Second - Passport have validity for (4) four years from the date of issuance for those under the age of (15) fifteen years.
Article 8 - there is no permitted to issue a passport for those under the age of (18) eighteen years without the guardian permession.
Article 9 - the form of laissez - passer is according model number (5) attached to this system and includes (16) sixteen page except for the cover.
Article -10 -
First - laissez - passer issued to: -
A - People who do not have citizenship and inside the Republic of Iraq.
B - Foreigners who wish to leave Iraq and the duration of their passports expired or they have lost it and no representative of their government in the Republic of Iraq have the authority to give them a document saying they can leave Iraq.
C - Iraqis living abroad who have lost or damaged their passports.
Second - the applicant of laissez - passer must attend in front of the competent passports officer and fill a form prepared for this purpose.
Article 11 - the laissez - passer shall be valid for a period of (6) six months from the date of issuance and the carrier of laissez - passer can enter or leave Iraq once to the mentioned countries only, and Diplomat in the Iraqi mission abroad when necessary to add other countries and enter the names of other people.
Article -12 - the official border crossing points used for leaving or entering the Republic of Iraq.
Article -13 - checking the documents related to travel for those entering or leaving the Republic of Iraq in residency centers and passports offices in the official border crossing points.
Article 14 - the residents in the border areas don't have to obtain a passport or laissez - passer regarding their crossing border with the following of border controls instructions prescribed by the Director of nationality and foreigners' residence Law No. (118) for the year 1987
Or any other law to replace it.
Article -15 -
First - the following persons gives service passport: -
A - Directors-General. 
B - The judges and prosecutors of categories I and II.
C - Army officers, internal security forces and intelligence from the rank of colonel and above.
D - Technical and Administrative staff in the Ministry of Foreign Affairs and Technical Attaches and their spouses and children who are legally dependents and residing with them.
E - Special Ranks those who retired before the issuance of this regulate, retirees according items (A) and (B) of this article and retired officers of the army, security forces procedure and intelligence of rank (Brigadier) and above .
Second - Foreign Minister authorized to grant a service passport for those who the nature of their work requires that or the public interest. 

Article -16 -
First - the following persons and their spouses and children who legally dependents and residents with them granted a special passport: -
A - The owners of Special Ranks who occupy their posts authenticity, they retaining them after their retired.
B - Retirees who was running the post of Minister after 9/4/2003.
Second - Foreign Minister according to regulations can grant a special passport for those who the nature of their work requires or the public interest.
Article -17 -
First - a diplomatic passport granted to the following persons who occupy their posts authenticity and their spouses and children who legally dependents and residents with them.
A - President of the Republic of Iraq.
B - Chairman of the Council of Ministers (prime Minister).
C - Chairman of the House of Representatives (Speaker of Parliament).
D - The Head of the Supreme Judicial Council.
E - The Region's President.
F - Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the region.
G - Vice- President of the Republic.
H - Deputy- Chairman of the Council of Ministers (Deputy Prime Minister).
I - Vice- President of the Federal House of Representatives (Speaker's Deputies).
J - Deputy- Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the region (Deputy Prime Minister of the region).
K - Ministers of the federal government and the regional government.
L - Person who hold the rank of Minister in the federal government.
M - Members of the House of Representatives (Parliament Members).
N - Secretary General of the Council of Ministers, the Chief of Staff of the Presidency of the Republic and President of the Office of the House of Representatives.
O - Advisers to the Presidency and the Council of Ministers.
P - The staff of the diplomatic corps at the Ministry of Foreign Affairs and technicians AttachÃ©s and their assistants.
Q - Secretary Generals and their assistants from Iraq who working in the Arab and International Organizations.
Second - Retirees referred to in paragraphs (a - f) of the first part of this article who hold office after 9/4/2003, and their spouses and children who are legally dependents and residing with them keeps their diplomatic passports, passport grants mentioned earlier. 
Third -Persons who retired after the activation of this regulations included in items from (A - L) and items (N) and (O) of paragraph (First) of this Article, who Occupying posts authenticity and Ambassadors and their spouses and children who legally residing with them keeps their diplomatic passports, and a diplomatic passport granted, for those who did not get the aforementioned passport. 
Fourth - A Diplomatic passport given to the holder of diplomatic courier commissioned by the Ministry of Foreign Affairs. 
Fifth - Minister of Foreign Affairs in accordance with the regulations granted a diplomatic passport for those who the nature of their work requires it or the public interest.

Article -18 - diplomatic passport, special passport and service passport not grant for the first time only in Iraq.
Article -19 - Subject to the provisions of items (II and III) of Article (17) of this system, drawing diplomatic passport or your service or who have the capacity in which authorized him the right to campaign and passport officer heroes.
Article -20 - Persons who are included in the provisions of Justice and Accountability Law No. (10) For the year 2008 and staffs of the repressive security agencies during the reign of the former regime are not included in this regulate.
Article -21 - The passport regulation No. (61) For the year 1959 was cancelled.
Article -22 - Minister of the Interior can issue instructions to facilitate the implementation of the provisions of the law.
Article -23 - This regulation is implemented from the date of publication in the Official Gazette.
