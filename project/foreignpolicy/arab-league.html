Arab League


  • Iraq was one of the founding members of the Arab League in 1945. In September 2003, a delegation from the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, led by Minister Zebari, regained Iraq's seat at the 120th Session of the Arab League in Cairo.

  • In March 2004, Minister Zebari also participated in the Arab League Foreign Minister's preparatory meeting in Cairo for the Arab Summit in Tunis, which will now be held on 22-23 May 2004.

  • Since 1980, under the former regime, Iraq failed to pay its dues to the Arab League. In March 2004, the Ministry of Foreign Affairs paid Iraq's annual membership totalling $3, 515, 411 to the organisation.

  • Iraq is fully committed to upholding and defending the charter of the Arab League and playing a vital role in all its agencies and institutions. We place great emphasis on strengthening multi-lateral relations in the Arab and Islamic domains and we actively promote friendly and beneficial relations between the Arab world and the international community.

Iraq’s Participations in the Meetings of Arab League on the Summit Level and the Arab Foreign Ministers Level


1- March 2007: H.E Foreign Affairs Minister, Hoshyar Zebari, heads the Iraq’s Delegation participating in the 127th Ordinary Session held in Cairo. The Meeting discussed the issues listed on the Agenda, to include the item of Iraq’s Situation Developments.


2- March 2007: H.E Foreign Affairs Minister, Hoshyar Zebari, participates in the 19th Ordinary Session of Arab Summit Conference in Riyadh. Iraq’s Delegation submitted many proposals on amending and updating the draft resolution on the situation in Iraq, in light of the results of conferences, meetings and political activities took place after the issuance of the resolution on Iraq.

3- July 2007: H.E Foreign Affairs Minister, Hoshyar Zebari, heads the Iraq’s Delegation participating in the Extraordinary Meeting of the Arab League Council on the Ministerial level held in Cairo. The Meeting discussed the findings of Arab efforts to activate the Arab Peace Initiative and the developments of the issue of the Libyan children injected with HIV.

4- September 2007: H.E Foreign Affairs Minister, Hoshyar Zebari, participates in the 127th Ordinary Session in Cairo. Through this session the item of Iraq’s Situation Developments was reviewed; where Iraq’s Delegation requested activating Clause 7 and 15 of the al-Khartoum Summit Resolution No. 340 on 29/03/2006 concerning the item of Iraq’s Situation Developments and put them into execution. Clause 7 stipulates that the member States, following the example of foreign States, shall have diplomatic representations in Baghdad soonest. Clause 15 stipulates urging the Arab States to cancel their debts on Iraq.

5- September 2007: H.E Foreign Affairs Minister, Hoshyar Zebari, heads the Iraq’s Delegation participating in the 128th Ordinary Session held in Cairo. Iraq’s Delegation succeeded in incorporating some amendments on the draft resolution prepared by the General Secretariat in line with the positive developments taking place in Iraq in various fields.

6- January 2008: H.E Foreign Affairs Minister, Hoshyar Zebari, heads the Iraq’s Delegation participating in the Extraordinary Meeting of the Arab League Council on the Ministerial level held in Cairo. The Meeting discussed the Lebanese crisis in light of the discords over the presidential elections in addition to the efforts exerted on activating the Arab Peace Initiative and countering the Israeli aggressive practices represented by the economic blockade and assaults on civilians in Gaza Sector.

7- On 27/01/2008, were held the meetings of the Extraordinary Session of the Arab League Council on Ministerial level. H.E. Foreign Affairs Minister, Hoshyar Zebari, headed Iraq’s Delegation to the Meeting, which was dedicated to follow up the developments of the situations in Lebanon and Palestine.

8- March 2008: H.E. Foreign Affairs Minister, Hoshyar Zebari, heads the Iraq’s Delegation participating in the 129th Ordinary Session in Cairo. Iraq’s Delegation succeeded in incorporating some amendments on the draft resolution prepared by the General Secretariat on the situation in Iraq.

9- March 2008: H.E. Foreign Affairs Minister, Hoshyar Zebari, participates in the 20th Session of Arab Summit in Damascus. The Summit issued the Special Resolution on Iraq, which included a reference to support the efforts of the Government of Iraq in rebuilding its security establishment on patriotic and professional basis, and create appropriate conditions to finalize the security issue across Iraq. In addition to the special condition on the situation of Iraqi emigrants in the Arab States, which included giving thanks and appreciation to the Arab States hosting Iraqi immigrants for the burdens borne by these States in securing decent living for them, and calling these States to facilitate granting visa and residence to Iraqi willing visitors, also to expedite the provision of urgent aids depositing them in the special account opened by the General Secretariat of Arab League to support the Iraqi emigrants.

10- May 2008: H.E. Foreign Affairs Minister, Hoshyar Zebari, heads the Iraq’s Delegation participating in the Extraordinary Meeting of the Council of Arab League on Ministerial level held in Cairo. The Meeting discussed the developments of the Lebanese crisis. H.E. delivered important interposition over the containment of the Lebanese crisis.

11- July 2008: The Foreign Affairs Undersecretary, Mr. Labeed Abbawi, acting for the Foreign Affairs Minister, heads the Iraq’s Delegation participating in the Extraordinary Meeting of the Arab League Council held in Cairo. The Meeting discussed the updates of the situation between Sudan and the International Criminal Court.

12- September 2008: H.E. Foreign Affairs Minister, Hoshyar Zebari, heads the Iraq’s Delegation participating in the 130th Ordinary Session held in Cairo. Iraq’s Delegation succeeded in incorporating some amendments on the draft resolution prepared by the General Secretariat of the Arab League (Iraq’s Situation Developments), which is a revised wording of the resolution adopted by the Arab League Council on Ministerial level in its previous 129th session.

13- March 2009: H.E. Foreign Affairs Minister, Hoshyar Zebari, heads the Iraq’s Delegation participating in the Meeting of the 131st Ordinary Session held in Cairo. New amendments and proposals were incorporated in the draft special resolution on Iraq, inter-alia, originating a new operative clause, Ser. 4th, over Iraq’s signing the Agreement on the US Troops Withdrawal by the end of 2011, as well as the Strategic Framework Agreement on Friendly Relation between the Republic of Iraq and the United States of America.

14- March 2009: H.E. Foreign Affairs Minister, Hoshyar Zebari, participates in the 21st Session of Arab Summit in Doha. H.E. Prime Minister Nouri al-Maliki delivered a comprehensive speech wherein he stressed on the importance of making the Doha Summit a comprehensive Arab outset. H.E. touched on Iraq’s success in surmounting the most dangerous crises and challenges threatened its unity and sovereignty. He reassured the Arab leaders that the Iraqi forces have become capable of filling the void will be left behind the US troops withdrawal from all Iraqi territories. H.E. reserved on the special wording on Iraq in the Doha Declaration; he requested the summit Headship to make amendments thereon. The request for amendment was approved, as per the wording submitted by H.E., to read as follows: (the Arab Summit praises the steps achieved in Iraq on security, stability, national unity, discarding sectarianism and stability of political process via the participation of the mosaic of Iraqi people). Also, he confirmed on Iraq’s right to host the next Arab Summit. However, due to incompletion of technical and logistic procedures to host this important Arab event, it has been decided that Iraq shall host the 23rd Session of Arab Summit in 2011.

15- May 2009: The Foreign Affairs Undersecretary, Mr. Labeed Abbawi, acting for the Foreign Affairs Minister, heads the Iraq’s Delegation participating in the Extraordinary Meeting of the Arab League Council on Ministerial level held in Cairo. The Meeting discussed the Israeli breaches in Gaza, and following up the war crimes committed by the Israeli occupation forces on Gaza Sector.

16- June 2009: H.E. Foreign Affairs Minister, Hoshyar Zebari, heads the Iraq’s Delegation participating in the Extraordinary Meeting of the Arab League Council on Ministerial level held in Cairo. The Meeting discussed the new Arab stand after the latest address of the US President, Barak Obama.

17- September 2009: H.E. Foreign Affairs Minister, Hoshyar Zebari, heads the Iraq’s Delegation participating in the Meeting of the 132nd Ordinary Session held in Cairo. New amendments and proposals were incorporated in the draft special resolution on Iraq. As well as praising the advancement taking place in the political process in Iraq and the Government’s role in anchoring and consolidation of the democracy, and holding the parliament and presidential elections in Kurdistan Region on 25/07/2009 with the participation of an Arab League’s delegation within the observation teams.

18- March 2010: The Foreign Affairs Undersecretary for Political Planning and Bilateral Relations, Mr. Labeed Abbawi, heads the Iraq’s Delegation participating in the 133rd Ordinary Session held in Cairo. New Clause was added to the draft special resolution on Iraq; where a clause concerning water and Iraq’s right to utilize its share, according to the international laws, was incorporated (Clause 14). Also, a new item concerning the situation of the displaced Iraqis in the hosting Arab countries was incorporated in accordance with Syrian draft and after making the necessary amendments resulted from the interpositions of the Iraq’s Delegation Head; where its wording was precise and objective.

19- March 2010: H.E. Foreign Affairs Minister, Hoshyar Zebari, heads the Iraq’s Delegation participating in the 22nd Ordinary Session of Arab Summit in Sirt – Libya, where the Resolution on the Situation in Iraq was passed according to the wording submitted by the Iraq’s Delegation and to serve the higher interests of Iraq. While the item of Displaced Iraqis in the Hosting Arab countries, was only included in the Agenda of the 133rd Session held in March 2010 in the General Secretariat’s headquarter in Cairo, from which Sirt Declaration was issued to hail the parliament elections took place in Iraq on 07/03/2010 and call Iraq to form the new government soonest. As well, the Meeting issued Sirt Document under which was formed a pentagonal committee to survey the reality of the Joint Arab Action, to which Iraq is a member represented by H.E. President Jalal Talabani.

 20- June 2010: Iraq participates in the Extraordinary Ministerial Meeting held in Cairo, where the following was discussed:
- Gaza Flotilla Raid, which was condemned.
- The Ministerial Meeting of the Pentagonal Higher Committee to discuss the Development of Joint Arab Action Group

21- June 2010: H.E. President Jala Talabani heads the Iraq’s Delegation participating in the Meeting of the Higher Pentagonal Committee held in Tripoli, capital of Libya. The development of the Joint Arab Action Group was discussed.

22- October 2010: H.E. President Jala Talabani heads the Iraq’s Delegation participating in the Extraordinary Arab Summit and the Afro-Arab Summit, both held in Sirt – Libya. The following meetings were held:
a- The Preparatory Meeting for the Afro-Arab Summit on the level of higher executives.
b- The Preparatory Meeting of the Arab Foreign Ministers.
c- The Preparatory Meeting of the Arab and African Foreign Ministers.
d- The Extraordinary Arab Summit.
e- The Afro-Arab Summit.
The Extraordinary Arab Summit discussed the following two items: The development of Joint Arab Action Group and the Arab Neighbourly Politics and Creating the Regional Neighbourly League. The discussions were dominated by controversial issues connected with the above mentioned items.


23.March 2011, Foreign Minister Hoshyar Zebari, headed the delegation of the Republic of Iraq to the regular session (135) to discuss the meeting of the Arab league at the summit level, which is scheduled to be held in Baghdad, and a decision was made to hold the summit in Baghdad May 15, 2011.


24.March 2011, Undersecretary Labeed, Abbawi headed the delegation of the Republic of Iraq and Ambassador Dr. Jaber Habib Jaber, Ambassador Mohammed Humaimidi to the extraordinary ministerial meeting, which dealt with the latest developments of events in Libya and its implications.


25. September 2011 Foreign Minister Hoshyar Zebari headed the delegation of the Republic of Iraq to the regular session (136) at the ministerial level at the General Secretariat headquarters in Cairo, the meeting focused on the situation in Syria.


26. October 2011, Iraq participated in the ministerial emergency meeting on the situation in Syria, which resulted in the issuance of a decision to form a committee of the Ministry headed by Qatar and Algeria, Oman, Sudan and the Secretary General of the League, to connect to the Syrian government and its claim to stop the violence in Syria.


27. November 2011, Iraq participated in the extraordinary session of the Arab League at the ministerial level to follow the latest developments in Syria.


28. November 2011, Iraq participated in the extraordinary session of the League of Arab States at the ministerial level to follow up the developments of the situation in Syria; the meeting was issued by Resolution No. (7438) which stated. - Suspend the participation of Syrian delegation in the meetings of the League of Arab states until the implementation of the Arab Plan .
- Provide protection for Syrian civilians
- Call on the Syrian army to not get involved in acts of violence and murder.
- The signing of economic sanctions against the Syrian government.
-Call upon Arab states to withdraw their ambassadors from Damascus.
- Invite Syrian opposition parties to a meeting at the headquarters of the Arab eLague.

29.November 2011, Iraq participated in the extraordinary session of the Arab League at the ministerial level to follow up on the situation in Syria, and stressed reservation Iraq on the fourth paragraph of the draft resolution (7441), on the imposition of economic sanctions on Syria in the absence of Syrian government signed the protocol of the Arab initiative.

30. November 27, 2011, Iraq participated in the extraordinary session of the Council of the League of Arab States at the ministerial level on the developments of the situation in Syria and that it issued Resolution No. (7442), containing the imposition of economic sanctions on Syria, which included a stop flights to Syria and stop commercial exchanges with the Syrian government In addition to freeze financial assets.

31. December 2011, Iraq participated in the Ministerial Committee on Syria, under the chairmanship of the State of Qatar, which reported the results of a list of names of officials that will be prevented from entering the Arab countries and freezing their assets.

32.The Meeting of the extraordinary session of the Arab League Council at the ministerial level on 2/6/2012 in Doha, to discuss the developments of the situation in Syria, and the situation in Sudan, and the Palestinian issue. The Minister chaired the esteemed delegation of Iraq, and the membership Undersecretary Labeed Abbawi and Permanent Representative Dr. Qais al-Azzawi.

33.The meeting of extraordinary session of the Arab League at the ministerial level took place on 22/7/2012 in Doha, to discuss the developments of the situation in Syria, the Palestinian issue, the Arab peace initiative. The meeting was chaired by Undersecretary Labeed Abbawi and the membership of Permanent Representative Dr. Qais al-Azzawi.
