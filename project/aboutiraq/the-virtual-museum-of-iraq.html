The Virtual Museum of Iraq

Visiting the Virtual Museum of Iraq


The National museum of Iraq is one of the important institutions in the world that established in 1932 in Alawi-al hilla baghdad,Iraq.



The Virtual Museum contains eight halls, set up in chronological order of Historical and cultural Eras like Assyrian, Sumerian, and Islamic era which contains different collections of Islamic Art.
