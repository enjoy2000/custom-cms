Economic Rehabilitation


Rehabilitation of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs:

    Legacy of the former regime ended with rickety institutions lacking efficiency due to the lack of scheme caused by the bad planned policies and individual decisions that expelled Iraqi state institutions away from the standards used by rest of the world.

     The Foreign Ministry, with its organizational structure and the management style, was part of that legacy.

     Iraq's new Government worked hard to make essential reform for the rehabilitation of the Foreign Ministry to show the bright image which befits the status of Iraq and its diplomatic history that the former regime has deformed through losing a lot of its luster giving the diplomatic institution a security touch that has dominated all of its departments, so the ministry and its structure was rehabilitated after 2003 to be harmonized with Iraq's pursuit to restore its active role through the constructive diplomatic work and the openness to the international community.

    The Iraqi Foreign Ministry worked and still working to achieve that vision in a thoughtful way through the following:

• Preparing  efficient diplomatic staff in the Foreign Service Institute according to the guidelines and precise conditions to choose, with high transparency, appropriate elements from the young Iraqi academics wishing to serve their country  in the diplomatic corps.

•  Providing modern curriculum to create diplomatic , administrative and technical staff capable  of practicing their jobs efficiently and in line with the evolution of the expanding Iraqi diplomatic work.

•  Successes of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs in the new Iraq went beyond its diplomatic duties to develop its legal systems that keep pace with the modern era. Foreign Service Law No. 45 of 2008 was issued to be a mainstay in determining the mechanisms of work within the organizational structure of the Iraqi diplomacy institution to grant it more disciplined flexibility, and by virtue of that low , a system of Foreign Service no. (1) of 2010 was also issued to clarify the practical purposes of the Foreign Service Law . To complete the ministry's pursuit for making the work more sophisticated and flexible, the ministry issued a booklet on its instructions to address the practical difficulties that appear during all types of the ministerial workflow.
