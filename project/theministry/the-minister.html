The Minister




Name: Dr. Ibrahim al-Jaafari

  • Iraqi Foreign Ministry.

  • Former Iraqi Prime Minister.

Date

Curriculum Vitae

1947Ibrahim  Al Ashaiqer, surnamed (Jaafari), Foreign Minister,born in the holy city of Karbala.
1966Joined the Islamic Dawa Party.
1966Got the highest secondary school grade at Mosul City that enabled him to join the Faculty of Medicine, University of Mosul.
1974Mr. Jaafari got married, with two sons and three daughters.
1980February, Left Iraq with his family, heading to Syria, and from there to Iran until 1990.
1980He was elected a member of the Islamic Dawa Party.
1982Participated in founding the Islamic Supreme Council, and be in charge of the Executive Office and the Executive Committee.
1990Resided at London until 2003.
1991Participated in the formation and leadership of (The Joint Action Committee of the Iraqi Opposition).
1991Participated in the Iraqi political conferences, such as the (Beirut Conference).
1992Participated in the formation and leadership of the (United National Congress).
1996Elected officially as a Spokesman for the Islamic Dawa Party.
2002Called to form the (Coalition of the Iraqi National Powers) which includes 17 political forces, along with 33 Iraqi figures representing the (Plenary Session).
20032003 After the fall of Saddam's regime, in August 2003, Dr. Jaafari became the first president of the Governing Council. The most prominent achievements were: forming a 25 members committee to prepare the draft new Iraqi constitution. He set up basis for the political relations of the new Iraq and visited seven Arab countries in seven days, in addition to his visit to the League of Arabic States in Cairo, where he delivered a distinctive speech. He formed the first Iraqi government in the new testament.
2004Dr. Ibrahim Al Jaafari occupied the Vice-President post of the Republic of Iraq. He contributed effectively to enhance compatibility and smooth flow in government work between the presidency and the cabinet. He found in his work as Vice-President a chance to enhance general Iraq's relations and Iraqi - Arab relations in particular.
2005occupied the Prime Minister post, as the first elected Prime Minister of Iraq, following the general elections which the Iraqi people participated in heavily January 30, 2005. He managed to achieve significant achievements during the transitional government time, by starting the wide development projects; to promote collapsed infrastructure, overcome the heavy legacy of the previous regime, in addition to the creation of the preparation and approval requirements of the (permanent constitution) of Iraq, and the successful election the permanent (House of Representatives) in under successful security, as well as paying great attention to the media, poets, artists, writers and athletes. He also laid the foundations of the rule of law and contributed to the consolidation of the foundations of the new Iraqi state.
20062006 Jaafari became a member of the House of Representatives for two terms; 2006 until 2010, and 2010 until 2014, in addition to the presidency of the National Alliance.
20082008 He announced the establishment of the National Reform Trend on May 31, 2008 and participated in the first provincial elections on January 2009.
20102010 He was elected as President of the Iraqi National Alliance, played a pivotal role in the convergence of views and solving political differences, and the formation of the Iraqi government.
2014Foreign Minister, Incumbent, in the government of Dr. Haider Al Abadi.

Has several books, studies, and researches, including (State Approach),(Experience of Rule),(Woman .. the Battle of Identity),(Art .. Pulse of Life),(Youth Between Authenticity and Innovation),(Students Makers of Life).
Several books were written about him: (Ring of Fire),(Iraqi Labor),(For A Better Tomorrow),(Kings and Message).
